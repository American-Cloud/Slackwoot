🚀 Quick Setup

1. Configure your mappings in config.yaml or via environment variables (SLACKWOOT_MAPPING_1, etc.)
2. Set your SLACK_BOT_TOKEN, SLACK_SIGNING_SECRET, CHATWOOT_API_TOKEN.
3. Point your Chatwoot webhook to the URL above.
4. Point your Slack App Events URL to the Slack events URL above.
5. Replies in Slack threads automatically flow back to Chatwoot conversations. 🎉
